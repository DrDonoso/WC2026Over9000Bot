## Summary

Investigated clip volume / disk pressure during live matches with missing keyboards and occasional goal-notification delays.

### Findings

**Clip Storage:**
- Location: `/app/state/clips/` (named volume `bot_state`)
- Per-goal metadata in `/app/state/goal_clips.json`

**Retention Policy:**
- Prune age: 7 days (every 45 seconds)
- No explicit size cap
- Clips NOT deleted after send (multiple users can tap same button)

**Disk Pressure Estimate:**
- Typical: 12–16 goals/day × 7 days retention = 84–112 clips
- Size estimate: 10–30 MB per clip
- **Expected range: 800 MB – 3.3 GB**
- Current free: ~4 GB → borderline but sufficient

### Conclusion

**Disk-full is unlikely PRIMARY cause** of missing keyboards. More likely: clip finder couldn't locate goal on Reddit, download failed silently, or poll job was slow. Kanté's delete-after-send feature (new) will reduce steady-state disk usage going forward.

### Recommendations

1. Monitor volume with disk-usage alert at <1 GB free.
2. Consider reducing prune age from 7 to 2 days for clips never pressed.
3. Verify `prune_old_entries` is running and deleting files successfully.
