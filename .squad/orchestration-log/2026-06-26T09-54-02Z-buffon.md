### Step 5: Design Limitation Identified

**Token collision in reconcile restart multi-goal:** When reconcile emits N deltas for N missed goals, all carry `new_home/new_away` final score. **Frequency:** Rare (bot restart + 2+ home goals missed at same score).

**Regression guard:** `test_restart_catchup_deltas_carry_final_score` added to document behavior.
