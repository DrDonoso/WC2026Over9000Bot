## Summary

**Root cause:** Both goal detectors (Reddit match-thread & football-data.org API) shared a single `bot_data["live_scores"]` dict. When one detector lagged, its lower score was misread as a decrease → false disallowed goal notification, triggering a loop.

**Solution:** Introduced per-source in-memory `seen_scores` (API and thread each maintain their own baseline) while keeping the single persisted `announced` score. New pure function `reconcile(seen, announced, new_home, new_away)` in `reddit/score_state.py` detects disallowed goals ONLY when the SAME source's own seen drops below announced (real VAR), never when a lagging source reports a lower value than announced.
