### Fix A: Streamff Mirror CDN Routing

**Problem:** Only `streamff.link` and `streamff.com` routed to CDN; other mirrors (`.pro`, `.gg`, `.one`, `.top`) fell through to yt-dlp causing "Unsupported URL".

**Solution:**
- Broadened `STREAMFF_CDN_ID_RE` from `streamff\.(?:com|link)` → `streamff\.[a-z]+/v/` (any TLD)
- Host guard changed from two explicit checks to `"streamff." in media_url`
- CDN resolution remains TLD-agnostic: `https://cdn.streamff.one/{id}.mp4`

**Verification:** Coordinator confirmed live download of `streamff.pro` + `streamff.gg` clips (27MB via CDN).

### Fix B: Thread-Based Early Goal Detection with Race-Free Dedup

**Problem:** football-data.org polls every 60s introduce lag. Reddit match threads post goals sooner but risk double-notification when football-data later confirms same score.

**Solution:**
- Shared in-memory score state: `build_app()` pre-loads `live_scores.json` into `app.bot_data["live_scores"]`
- Both `poll_goals_job` and `poll_thread_goals_job` read/write same dict
- When thread job notifies and updates score, football-data diff returns `[]` on next tick → no duplicate
- New `_notify_goal` helper extracted from goal-delta logic; used by both jobs
- Thread scorer used directly (no OpenAI enrichment = speed win)

**Verification:** Coordinator confirmed live notification of England 3-2 vs Croatia from thread, earlier than football-data, with shared score dedup working correctly.
